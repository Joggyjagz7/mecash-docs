# Get Started

## Quick start with mecash

## Step 1: Create a Business Account

To access the MeCash API, you must first create a business account.

1. Visit the [MeCash Signup Page](#) (me-cash.com).
2. Select **Business Account** and click on **Sign Up**.
<Bleed>![business signup](/Screenshot203328.png)</Bleed>

3. You will be redirected to the "Create your MeCash business account" page.

<Bleed>![create business account](/Screenshot090648.png)</Bleed> 

4. Fill out the registration form with the following details:
   - First Name
   - Last Name
   - Business Name
   - Business Email Address
   - Password (and confirm it)
5. Click **Sign Up** to create your account.

6. A verification email will be sent to your registered email address. Click the link in the email to verify your account.

## Step 2: Complete KYC Verification

After setting up your business profile, you need to complete the KYC (Know Your Customer) verification process.

1. Navigate to the **KYC Verification** section on your dashboard.
2. Click on **Complete Setup Now**.
<Bleed>![verify business](/Screenshot210630.png)</Bleed> 

3. You will be directed to the **Business Contact Form Page**. Fill out the required personal information.
<Bleed>![business contact form](/Screenshot212131.png)</Bleed>

4. After that, you will be directed to the **Business Details Page**. Here, you will need to upload the required documents, such as:
   - Business registration certificate
   - Valid ID
   - Proof of address
<Bleed>![business details](/Screenshot212359.png)</Bleed>

5. Submit your documents for review.
6. Wait for approval. You will be notified once your KYC verification is successfully completed.

## Step 3: Generate Your API Key

Once your KYC verification is complete, you can generate your API key.

1. Go to the **Developer** section in your dashboard.
<Bleed>![developer section](/Screenshot100106.png)</Bleed> 

2. Click on **Create a Key**.
<Bleed>![create api key](/Screenshot084241.png)</Bleed>

3. Your API key will be automatically generated.
<Bleed>![api key generated](/Screenshot084315.png)</Bleed> 

4. Make sure to copy and securely store your API key for future use.

## Next Steps

If you encounter any issues or need further assistance, don't hesitate to reach out to MeCash support.
